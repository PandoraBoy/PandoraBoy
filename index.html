PandoraBoy by PandoraBoy

Pandora.com on your mac ... without a web browser.

Love Pandora.com, but tired of having to run it a web browser? Ever wish you could control pandora without even being at your computer? Well then look no further. PandoraBoy allows you to control your music with global hotkeys or your apple remote. Additionally, it supports growl notifications so that you always know what you're listening to.
